Your Matchmaking Team

Meet your matchmaking team. We have a combination of over 100 years of work experience in the matchmaking industry. Our Founder has over 30 years of matchmaking experience and a special gift for bringing the right people together. Our Client Concierge and Client Services Specialist are very hands on and are here to serve you. The entire team works great together and provides successful results for our members. You will be contacted by different team members providing services tailored to your needs. We are here to help you, so reach out to us at any time.

Founder – Jennie Rowe

Meet Jennie! Jennie has been a matchmaker for over 30 years. She understands the needs of singles and knew she had to start her own matchmaking company in order to meet those needs. What started with a team of 2 and a database of 50 members, has gone on to become a team of 6 with thousands of members. Jennie continues to look for new ways to fulfill the needs of the members on their quest for their happily ever after. She loves that she can make the rules, but also play by them. Matchmaking is her first love. She also enjoys bike-riding, hiking and spending time with her grandkids.

President – Tamika Reynolds

Meet Tamika! Tamika believes in fairytale love. Once a client of “I Do,” She now handles a good portion of the business matters. Her work history includes experience at a Fortune 500 company, where she realized that she didn’t have time to pursue the lovelife she desired. Today, her position as President allows her to help others find love and it also provides a good work-life balance. Tamika now has the time she needs to foster her own relationship. She likes working with numbers and is very business oriented. She also enjoys reading, running and vacationing with her wife.

Executive Matchmaker – Kim Nguyen

Meet Kim! Kim is one of our original employees. She met the founder, Jennie, at a matchmaking conference and they’ve worked together ever since. She really believes in the “I Do” mission and business model. Though she loves working with everyone, she made it her special mission to increase the male-seeking-female membership. She believes that this is an underrepresented area of singles that deserve to be loved, as well. Kim is happy to engage in outreach activities and go the extra mile. She will gladly officiant your wedding service, if asked to do so. She enjoys shopping, karaoke, great restaurants, and getting together with friends.

Meet Martina! Martina is the fresh face of matchmaking here at “I Do” and we’re ecstatic to have her.
